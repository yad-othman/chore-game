🎮 How It Works

  • Each chore has fixed credits — you cannot change them
  • Prove it with before & after photos or video
  • Task is logged and stays Pending until mum approves
  • Credits are totalled at end of the month
  • Money is given based on total approved credits only

⚖️ Fairness Rules

  • No credit without proof
  • Bad cleaning = redo the task, no credits
  • Tasks rotate so nobody always does the same
  • Helping mum voluntarily = bonus credits
  • Tap the proof tag in History to approve entries
